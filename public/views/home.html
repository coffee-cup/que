Que

Shareable Music Queue

Create Queue
As simple as clicking a button. They are free to make so make as many as you want!
Add Music
Search for your favorite songs or artists and add tracks to the queue.
Share Link
Copy your unique url and give it to your friends, enemies, or... anybody. Each queue will have the same music and play it at the same time.
